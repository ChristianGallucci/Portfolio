Boca Juniors US Tour: New Jersey

by Christian Gallucci

7/29/19

It had been a long time since I've seen my favorite soccer team play live. Last time must have been about 12 or so years ago when I first stepped foot in La Bombonera and witnessed an amazing free kick goal from Juan Roman Riquelme against Thiago Silva's Fluminense. Nothing could ever compare to that moment. The feeling you get from the moment the ball nestles into the back of the net, in that crowd, in that stadium, is something 18 year old me never got from watching them on TV.
